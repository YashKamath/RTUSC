What is Review Team?

Who can submit essays?
Undergraduate students all across campus can submit their essays through our streamlined website, which allows for effective and efficient reviewer-reviewee feedback within hours of submission.
Who can review essays?
Only reviewers who are majoring in the humanities, have extensive writing experience, and have completed our mandatory testing and training process are allowed to review essays.
How long does the whole essay submission, editing, and return process take?
Within five minutes, the student will be able to navigate through their portal and submit their essay for review. By the end of the day, they will have a completely commented, reviewed essay which they can build upon as they continue their essay writing process.
What types of writing are allowed? Review Team will accept any type of writing (resume, personal statement, academic essay, research paper, creative writing) as long as it is under 10 pages in length.

How much does the service cost?
Our costs are determined by the length of your document in words. The longer the writing, the higher the cost.
What is the essay return time?
We offer 4 distinct return time options for our buyers:
  • RUSH - 6 hour return
  • STANDARD - 24 hour return
  • LONG - 48 hour return
  • PREPARED - Week long return
The return time will not affect the quality of your essay feedback. A RUSH review will have the same high quality feedback that you would receive if you ordered a prepared review.
